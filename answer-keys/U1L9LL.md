## Grading Vocabulary for This Key

- **Circular reasoning / begging the question:** the reason assumes the conclusion or only restates it.
- **Independent support:** a reason that can be accepted without already granting the conclusion.
- **Loaded definition:** a definition that builds the verdict into the term.
- **Restatement:** the conclusion repeated in other words and offered as a reason.
- **Rewrite:** a non-circular version with testable support or a narrowed claim.
- **Shifting term / equivocation:** Week 8 review — one word, two senses.
- **Fallacy defect (Week 7 review):** where apparent proof fails as real support.

**Rubric:**

**Expected Answer:**

- 0 pts: Repeats trust language only
